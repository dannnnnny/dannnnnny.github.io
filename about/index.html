About DJ Party Pete

Hey, I’m Pete!

I have been involved with music professionally for 20 years now. I have worked in many schools and as a  dress up pirate for kindergarten and preschool functions, I am currently based in the Waikato but have been hired to play as far as Dargaville in the upper north island and Featherston in the lower north. So give me a call to see if I can come to your next event.

I’m passionate about all parties/functions but here are the most popular selection below

  • Weddings
  • Birthdays
  • Children Parties
  • Private Functions-these include microphone hire and party lighting including mirror ball.
  • School balls

Please get in contact to discuss anything further.
